About the Participants Linking your Participant data with NHS Digital’s data

Information:

NHS Digital use Master Person Service (MPS) to link Participant data with NHS Digital data. MPS helps NHS Digital increase the amount of useable, better-quality data available to support research and planning.

Guidance

When you provide NHS Digital with Participant data (that is, a list of identified individuals), you must include a Study ID that contains a unique and non-identifiable value for each individual. All Participant data submissions must be submitted as a .CSV file using the column headers and formats in the table.
