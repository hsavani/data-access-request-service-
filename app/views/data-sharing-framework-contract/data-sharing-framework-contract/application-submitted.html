What happens next

We will send you and the authorised signatory (Approver) an email confirming that we have received the application.

The application will be reviewed by NHS Digital’s Data Access Request Service (DARS), who will contact you within 7 days if further information is needed.

The contract will then be sent to the authorised signatory to sign. (You will also receive a copy of this email.)

If you would like to speak to someone about your application, email data.applications@nhsdigital.uk quoting your contract reference number in the subject field.
