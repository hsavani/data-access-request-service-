Requesting access to data

The Data Access Request Service (DARS) enables clinicians, researchers and commissioners to request the data they need to help improve NHS services.

Information:

Do not use this service if you wish to apply for tabulated or aggregated data with small numbers suppressed. Such requests should be made via enquiries@nhsdigital.nhs.uk quoting ‘DARS Tabulation Request – small numbers suppressed’ in the subject field.

You can use this service if the controller requesting the data:
  • wants to apply for record level data
  • has an active registration with the ICO under the UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Data will only be released under a Data Sharing Agreement (DSA) once the Controller organisation has a Data Sharing Framework Contact (DSFC) in place.

Before you start

Make sure you read:

  • application guidance (opens in a new window)
  • pre-application eligibility criteria (opens in a new window)
  • standards of information expected (opens in a new window)

Use the below links to help you understand:

  • what data sets (opens in a new window) you are requesting
  • what legal basis (opens in a new window)will apply
  • the charges (opens in a new window) that will apply for the data you are requesting

You can see some examples of applications that have been approved in our Data uses register (opens in a new window). These previously accepted applications provide examples of well-written answers to questions that will support your application process.

Not all requests for data will progress to the application stage. It may be that your requirement can be satisfied through existing published data or you don’t have the legal basis to access the data.

During your application

We’ll ask you for:

  • evidence that Controllers and Processors are complying with the minimum-security standards that support the legal bases required for your application
  • details on the purpose of your data access request
  • details on what will be produced as a result of data processing, along with the clearly stated benefits
  • information on how any output benefits the health and social care system
  • evidence of any funding
  • information on the organisations acting as the Controllers, Processors and data storage locations

If you need support with your request for data contact NHS Digital on 0300 303 5678 or email enquiries@nhsdigital.nhs.uk

After you’ve submitted your application

Our team will:

  • review your application
  • work with you to collect any missing details

The time that it takes for your application to be signed and agreed is dependent on the complexity of your data request.
