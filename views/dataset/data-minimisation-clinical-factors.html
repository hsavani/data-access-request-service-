Data set filters The clinical factors of the data you are requesting

Providing us with clinical-factor-based information enables NHS Digital to provide an appropriately filtered cohort from the data set for use in your research.

When providing details in the text box, think about what data you need and how it relates to the purpose. You should consider clinical factors that include but are not limited to:

  • comorbidity
  • diagnosis
  • drug allergies
  • medical history
  • prescribed medication
  • non-surgical therapy
  • operations and procedures
  • post-diagnosis survival rates
  • symptoms without diagnosis
  • treatment

When providing information of a diagnosis, operation or procedure use the Query Resolution Database (opens in a new window) to find the ICD-10 and OPCS codes and add them to the text box.

Where possible, use bullet points or short sentences to describe the required critical factors of the data you are requesting.
